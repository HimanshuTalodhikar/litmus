# ProjectFlow

## Overview
ProjectFlow is a task and sprint management tool built for engineering teams. It helps teams plan sprints, track progress, and ship faster.

## Features
- Sprint planning with capacity planning and story point estimation
- Gantt charts for timeline visualization and dependency tracking
- Time tracking with billable hours and automated timesheets
- Custom workflows with automation rules and triggers
- Real-time collaboration with live updates
- Integrates with GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket
- Burndown and burnup charts for sprint velocity

## Pricing
- **Free**: up to 5 users, 3 projects
- **Pro**: $12/user/month — unlimited projects, advanced reporting
- **Enterprise**: custom pricing — SSO, audit logs, dedicated support

## Status
Launched in Q1 2025. Current version: 2.3.1

## Integrations
Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Figma, Google Calendar, Zapier
